University CS60-499 Project Management: Techniques and Tools

Project Name: Coop System Prototype

Group Number: 5

Supervisor: Dr. J. Chen

Group member: Chih Yu Wu

The goal of this project is to develop a prototype of a coop management system. The project should identify and demonstrate the major functionality needed for an actual online coop system, which could be built to facilitate the management of coop programs at the University of Windsor.

This prototype may include components such as interview scheduling, employer evaluation, student report submission and grading, student presentation organization, coop term tracking, electronic grade submission, report generation, etc.
